The Operation
Bolivia

Hugo Banzer

Hugo Banzer was a former Bolivian president who presided over the country during Operation Condors. His repressive military regime is notable in the scope of Operation Condor.

"Banzer ordered 39 political assassinations and the military repression in his dictatorship caused 429 deaths. One of those executions was that of Colonel Andrés Selich, the dictator's right hand man, later charged with treason. Now, Banzer's successors accuse us of 'dictatorship'"

- Evo Morales, Former President of Bolivia
